Introduction

Stay informed with news and facts about coronavirus. During a pandemic, it is important to rely in on trusted resources to keep you informed. Founded by a physician, our site empowers patients by keeping them more informed. The functionality of our site will be evolving shortly with introduction of a mobile app and personalized website. New functionality will provide ability to subscribe to news feeds based on your geography around the world (with news notification via phone or email), links to trusted resources across the globe, and frequently asked questions.

Personalize your experience by subscribing to coronavirus news updates. Select your country and others across the globe. If you have loved ones and want to follow what is going on locally because you are afraid they won’t share the news, select that country and you will stay more informed
